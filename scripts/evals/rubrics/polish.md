# Visual polish (0-5)

Score the overall craft and finish of the slide: would this pass as a professionally designed deck slide? This is theme-agnostic; both the light and dark themes can look equally polished.

- The slide feels intentional and finished, not like a default template dump.
- Decoration (dot grid, soft glow, accent rules, icon circles, avatars, QR card)
  is used tastefully to support the content, never distracting or cluttered.
- Type sizing and weight create a pleasing rhythm; line lengths are comfortable;
  nothing is awkwardly large or cramped.
- Color accents (green, occasional gold) are placed deliberately to guide the eye,
  in the right proportion for the theme.
- Details are clean: crisp edges, even corner radii, aligned icons, no stray
  artifacts, consistent treatment across repeated elements (cards, stats, people).

5 = polished, deliberate, gallery-quality within the house style. 3 = competent but with a few rough or generic touches. 0 = visually rough, cluttered, or unfinished.

Return "Unknown" if there is too little on the slide to judge its craft.
