# Brand fidelity (0-5)

Score how well the slide image matches the VISEMI / Cất Cánh brand. This is theme-agnostic: the deck may use the light theme (pale gradient background, navy text) OR the dark theme (deep navy gradient background, white text). Both are correct. Judge against whichever theme the slide uses, not against a fixed background color.

- Palette is only the brand colors and their token shades: navy #001669, green
  #01B68B, gold #F5B433 (used sparingly as an accent), white, and the neutral   navy/blue/gray shades derived from them. Green is a secondary accent; gold is   rare. No off-brand hues (no red, magenta, orange, etc.) dominate.
- Typography reads as Be Vietnam Pro (a clean geometric sans-serif), not a serif
  or a generic system font.
- The VISEMI logo is present, uncorrupted, and on an appropriate background
  (the dark-theme white logo on dark, the color logo on light).
- Vietnamese keeps full diacritics ("Cất Cánh", never "Cat Canh") and the program
  name reads "Cất Cánh (Takeoff) Fellowship".
- No emojis; no em dashes or en dashes in the copy.

5 = indistinguishable from the house brand in its theme. 3 = mostly on-brand with minor slips. 0 = off-brand colors or fonts dominate, or brand text rules are broken.

Return "Unknown" if the image is unreadable or missing.
