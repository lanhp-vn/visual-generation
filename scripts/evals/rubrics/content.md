# Content fidelity to brief (0-5)

Score whether the slide faithfully presents the facts from the task brief and invents nothing. This is theme-agnostic: judge the text and figures, not the background.

- The key facts for this slide (names, titles, roles, figures, dates, dollar
  amounts, percentages, tier names, URLs) are present and correct exactly as   given in the task brief.
- No figures, claims, names, or program details are fabricated, padded, or
  altered from the brief.
- Text is complete and legible: not truncated mid-sentence, not clipped, and not
  reduced to placeholder filler.
- Vietnamese terms from the brief are reproduced verbatim with diacritics intact.

5 = every brief fact is present and correct; nothing invented or dropped. 3 = mostly correct with a minor omission or paraphrase. 0 = key facts missing, wrong, or fabricated.

Return "Unknown" if the brief facts cannot be checked from the image.
